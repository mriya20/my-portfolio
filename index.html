PawPals

PawPals is a pet adoption website designed to allow users to easily browse through a selection of adoptable pets based on selected filters (ex. breed, age, weight). Combined with a login feature, PawPals has an authentification method to insure that the user's application is affiliated with their own encrypted account. I had the privellege to work primarily on Back end functuionality, implementation of MVC paradigm, backend of login & sign up form, Agile Methodology and came up with project idea and made the logo. Stay up to date on the project by clicking the logo above!



Carrot Calendar

Gaining over $100k in revenue per year, Bobbys Rabbits needed a simple, efficient way to coordinate community events to boost interaction and sales. Carrot Calendar is a user-friendly calendar for users of Bobbys Rabbits to create online events, RSVP, and pitch money in the pot for games. I worked on the deployment of the weather API by your location, created the "Join us" button where you submit your user name and city, to see the current weather. HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap, APIs, Agile Methodology, DOM, and Node.js.

This project has been submitted to a Hackathon!
